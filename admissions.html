Admissions Guidelines

We welcome students from diverse backgrounds who are committed to academic excellence and personal growth. Review the admissions guidelines carefully and reach out if you have questions.

  • Interviews and entrance assessments may be required.
  • Previous academic records must be submitted.
  • All application forms must be submitted before the deadline.

Enrollment Procedure

  1. Complete the application form and gather necessary documents.
  2. Submit the application online or at the school office.
  3. Wait for confirmation and attend an interview if invited.
  4. Pay the admission fees and receive your student kit.
