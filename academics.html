Academic Excellence

Explore our diverse curriculum, departments, and co-curricular programs.

Curriculum Overview

Sunrise High School follows the Uganda National Examinations Board (UNEB) curriculum, supplemented with practical activities, research-based learning, and ICT integration.

Departments

Science Department

Offering Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Agriculture with fully equipped laboratories and practical sessions.

Mathematics Department

Focused on building strong analytical and problem-solving skills through applied mathematics.

Languages Department

English and Literature as core subjects, plus Kiswahili and French to promote communication skills.

Social Studies Department

Subjects include History, Geography, and Political Education, building cultural and global awareness.

Business Department

Courses in Entrepreneurship, Commerce, and Accounts prepare students for the business world.

ICT Department

Digital literacy, computer programming, and hands-on computing skills for the 21st century learner.

Co-Curricular Activities

We offer clubs and societies such as Debate, Scouts, Music, Drama, and various sports to support student growth beyond academics.
